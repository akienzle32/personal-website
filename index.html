Hello,

my name is Alec Kienzle /ˈælɪk ˈkɪnzli/, and I'm a PhD student in linguistics at the University of Toronto. I study morphosyntax and semantics, mostly with respect to verbs. I used to work in ancient Hebrew philology, and still occasionally dabble in it. My current research interests include:

  • Syntax and semantics of middle voice
  • Complex nominalizations
  • Applicatives in Cuzco Quechua
